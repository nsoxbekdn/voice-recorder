# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Personal use — a single developer/student who records audio while studying or working, then replays and reviews those recordings later.

## Product Purpose

A local-first browser-based voice recorder that saves audio files directly to disk. The primary task is quick capture: open, record, stop, done. Secondary task is review: browse past recordings, play back, rename, delete.

## Brand Personality

Focused, minimal, nocturnal. The interface should feel like a quiet studio tool — purposeful, not flashy.

## Anti-references

- No bubbly consumer audio apps (voice memos iOS aesthetic)
- No DAW complexity
- No SaaS chrome (sidebar nav, breadcrumbs, top bars with icons)

## Design Principles

1. Recording is the primary act — nothing competes with it visually when active
2. Controls must be reachable without searching — visibility over subtlety
3. State is always obvious — idle, recording, paused, saved each feel distinct
4. The UI disappears into the task — no decoration that doesn't earn its place

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. All interactive controls must be keyboard-reachable and have clear focus indicators.
